About The Candidate

David Lewis Rubongoya

David Lewis Rubongoya is a Ugandan lawyer, academic, and political activist. He is best known as the Secretary General of the National Unity Platform (NUP), Uganda's leading opposition party. Rubongoya has played a significant role in advocating for democracy, human rights, and good governance in Uganda.

Education:

  • Bachelor of Laws, Makerere University
  • Master of Laws, Harvard Law School
  • Diploma in Legal Practice, Law Development Centre

Career & Political Experience:

  • Lecturer, International Law at Uganda Christian University
  • Secretary General, National Unity Platform (NUP)
  • Prominent advocate for democracy and human rights in Uganda
